POSTER MAKING COMPETITION

Rules and Regulations

  1. The topic for the event is “ Sustainability ” where you have to design a poster online or draw the poster.

    Sub theme for sustainability :-

    • Sustainable energy
    • Innovations in sustainable technology
    • Sustainable transportation
  2. For Handmade poster : Artwork must be in A3 size sheet(22 inches in length and 15 inches in width). Which is going to be submitted in the form of jpg or pdf as well as in offline mode.
  3. For Digital poster : Artwork must be in the size of Instagram post (1080x1080 px). Which is going to be submitted in the form of jpg or pdf as well as with an editable link.
  4. Entries of original work will be accepted. Any copied work will not be considered and will result in disqualification.
  5. Submit the poster before the submission deadline after deadline no entries will be accepted. Submission form will be open after the registration form deadline.
  6. Winner and participants will get the prizes and certificate respectively.
